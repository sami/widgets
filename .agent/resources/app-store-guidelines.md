# App Store Guidelines (Apple)

## 1. Submission Checklist
-   **Icons**: All sizes generated in Asset Catalog.
-   **Screenshots**: Required for 6.5" (iPhone 11 Pro Max+) and 5.5" (iPhone 8 Plus) sizes.
-   **Privacy**: Updated Privacy Policy URL.
-   **Compliance**: "Use of Encryption" form answered.

## 2. Review Guidelines (Common Rejections)
-   **Guideline 2.1 - Performance**: App crashes on iPad. (Always test on iPad even if iPhone only).
-   **Guideline 4.0 - Design**: "Web View" apps that just wrap a website are rejected.
-   **Guideline 5.1.1 - Data**: Asking for data permission (e.g. tracking) before user logs in or interacts with feature.

## 3. TestFlight
-   **Internal Testing**: Instant deployment. Team members only.
-   **External Testing**: Requires Beta Review (1-2 days). For Public beta.

## 4. Metadata
-   **Promotional Text**: Can be updated without new binary.
-   **Keywords**: 100 characters max. Comma separated. No spaces.
