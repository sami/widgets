# Google Play Guidelines

## 1. Submission Checklist
-   **Bundle**: Upload `.aab` (Android App Bundle), not `.apk`.
-   **Feature Graphic**: 1024x500px image (Crucial for store presence).
-   **Content Rating**: Complete the IARC questionnaire.
-   **Data Safety**: Complete the Data Safety form (Privacy declaration).

## 2. Tracks
-   **Internal**: For QA/Dev team. Instant.
-   **Closed Testing**: Invite by email lists.
-   **Open Testing**: Public beta.
-   **Production**: The live store.

## 3. Review Process
-   **Time**: Automated + Manual. usually 1-3 days. Longer for new accounts.
-   **Update Rejection**: If an update is rejected, it doesn't remove the *existing* version from store usually.

## 4. Monetization
-   **Billing Library**: Must use Google Play Billing for digital goods.
-   **Subscriptions**: Ensure "Account Hold" and "Grace Period" are configured.
